One morning, when Higu Rose woke from troubled dreams, they found themself transformed in their bed into a horrible catboy...

higu rose is an illustrator, cartoonist, and local terror living in pittsburgh, PA. based in fiction and autobiography, higu’s work focuses on experiences of being black, trans, and mentally ill. their narratives are a constant endeavor to understand the self and society, with a snarling desire to love and live. one time they made Chicago mayor Rom Emmanual wait five minutes for a 8oz cup of coffee.

they work in ink, graphite, acryla gouache, some digital programs, and spit.
