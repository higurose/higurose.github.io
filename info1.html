higu rose is a black artist and resident terror of chicago, il. based in fiction and autobiography, their work focuses on experiences of being queer, isolated, and mentally ill. their narratives are a constant endeavor to understand the self and society, with a snarling desire to love and live. higu is the worst kind of cool guy. bad teeth. don't know math, neither.
